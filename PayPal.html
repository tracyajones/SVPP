Golden Paws Program
The companionship and devotion of a pet is something that brings joy to life. Golden Paws Program is designed to give our older animals the opportunity to be placed in a loving home by matching them with senior citizens who are 65 years +. An older animal would be a great choice for an older person that needs a buddy! Adoption fees are discounted 50%.

Adopt a Buddy Program

We realize the importance of having a buddy! Adopt a second pet from us at time of adoption (or within a month) and receive a discounted adoption fee. We believe it is emotionally healthy for animals to have a friend and have found that some behavioral issues can easily be solved or never occur with the addition of a second pet into a household. Second Adoption fee is 50% off.
