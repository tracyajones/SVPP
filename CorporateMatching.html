Corportate Matching

Many companies offer matching gift benefits to their employees, doubling the value of their charitable contributions. These donations make a critical difference for SVPP to carry out our mission.

Before you make your gift to SVPP, please check in the matching gift company search box below—or with your company's human resources or benefits office—to see if your employer has a corporate matching gift program that will make your support go twice as far.

The matching gift process differs from company to company. Some require a completed paper acknowledgment form, while others have an online system. Others still request that claims be filed by telephone. In any case, if your company does provide a matching gift claim form, please complete it and send it to:

Silicon Valley Pet Project
P.O. Box 6145
San Jose, CA 95150

Some companies require signed originals, but others may accept faxed forms. Please check with your employer to be sure before you fax your claim form to SVPP at 408.610.4020.

For all other matching gift questions, please contact:
donate@svpetproject.org
